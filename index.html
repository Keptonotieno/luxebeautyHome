Our Story

About LUXES

Celebrating Luxes Beauty Since 2019

LuxesBeauty was born out of a passion to create a space where African beauty is celebrated,- enhanced, and showcased in all its glory. Founded in 2019 in the heart of Nairobi, our salon has become a sanctuary for those seeking premium beauty services with an authentic African touch.

Our mission is to provide exceptional beauty services that honor African heritage while incorporating modern techniques and products. We believe that beauty is diverse and should be approached with cultural understanding and respect.

Every treatment at LuxesBeauty is performed with the highest quality products, many of which feature traditional African ingredients known for their nourishing and healing properties. Our team of skilled professionals is trained to address the specific needs of African skin and hair, ensuring that every client leaves feeling confident and beautiful.
